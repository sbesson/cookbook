Spinach SaladÂ¶

Serves 3

Ingredients

  • Organic spinach,
  • 1 avocado,
  • 2 tomatoes,
  • 1 apple or pear,
  • some sort of nuts,
  • optional: cheese,
  • whatever else looks good that day at Berkeley Bowl or other supermarket.

For the dressing

  • cup balsamic vinegar,
  • 2 tbsp Dijon mustard,
  • 1 tbsp minced garlic,
  • 1 tsp freshly ground black pepper,
  • 1 tsp salt,
  • 1 cup plus 2 tablespoons olive oil.
  • Optional: hint of parmesan.

Preparation

Stir everything but the olive oil, then whisk the mixture continuously while slowly pouring in the oil. Serve with the salad.
