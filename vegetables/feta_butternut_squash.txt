Feta and butternut squash bake
==============================
*Serves 4*

.. rubric:: Ingredients

- 500 g :index:`butternut squash` cut in cubes,
- 200 g :index:`green beans`,
- 100 g :index:`feta <cheese; feta>`,
- 15-20 black olives,
- 2 tbsp olive oil.

.. rubric:: Preparation

Place the butternut squash with olive oil in an ovenproof dish. Roast it in 
the oven preheated to 180Â°C for 30 minutes. Boil the green beans for 5 minutes 
and drain. Chop the feta cheese into small cubes. Add the feta, green beans 
and olives to the butternut squash in the serving dish.
