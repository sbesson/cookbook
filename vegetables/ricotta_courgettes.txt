Ricotta stuffed courgettes
==========================
*Serves 4*

.. rubric:: Ingredients

- 4 :index:`courgettes`,
- 2 spring onions finely chopped or a few leaves of basil,
- 250 g :index:`ricotta cheese <cheese; ricotta>`,
- 50 g toasted pine nuts,
- juice of 1 :index:`lemon`,
- 2 tbsp olive oil,
- salt & pepper.

.. rubric:: Preparation

Thinly slice the courgettes lengthwise, using a swivel peeler. Place the 
slices in a tray and brush with olive oil. Chill for 20 minutes. Mix the 
ricotta with the lemon juice and seasoning to taste, then fold the chopped 
basil and pine nuts. Place a teaspoon of ricotta cheese mix on one end of the 
courgette strip and roll up. Repeat to use up all the filling. Arrange the 
courgette cannelloni on a plate and grind over some black pepper. Drizzle with 
a little olive oil and balsamic vinegar and serve.
