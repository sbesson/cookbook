Spinach Salad
=============
*Serves 3*

.. rubric:: Ingredients

- Organic :index:`spinach`,
- 1 :index:`avocado <avocados>`,
- 2 :index:`tomatoes`,
- 1 :index:`apple <apples>` or pear,
- some sort of nuts,
- optional: :index:`cheese`,
- whatever else looks good that day at Berkeley Bowl or other supermarket.

.. rubric:: For the dressing

- cup balsamic vinegar,
- 2 tbsp Dijon mustard,
- 1 tbsp minced garlic,
- 1 tsp freshly ground black pepper,
- 1 tsp salt,
- 1 cup plus 2 tablespoons olive oil.
- Optional: hint of parmesan.

.. rubric:: Preparation

Stir everything but the olive oil, then whisk the mixture continuously while 
slowly pouring in the oil. Serve with the salad.
