French bean soufflé¶

Serves 3 or 2 hungry footballers

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs,
  • 100 g comté or cheddar,
  • 200 g green beans,
  • 40 cL milk,
  • 50 g butter,
  • 40 g flour,
  • thyme,
  • salt & pepper.

Preparation

Pre-heat the oven to 210°C.

Boil the green beans for 10-12 minutes. Drain them and chop them into small segments. Melt 50 g butter in a pan, add the flour and cook for 1 minute. Add the milk slowly, stirring constantly until it thickens. Add the cheese, salt, black pepper and thyme.

Beat the egg whites. When the sauce has cooled down, add the egg yolks and the green beans, and incorporate the egg whites. Pour into a greased dish or individual moulds and bake for 15 minutes. Serve as soon as it is cooked, with a French bean salad and vinaigrette.
