Savory cakes¶

Serves 6-8

Ingredients

  • 170 g flour,

  • 3 eggs,

  • 100 mL milk,

  • 75 mL olive oil,

  • 100 g grated cheese,

  • 1 tsp baking powder and any good ingredients.

Preparation

Mix the flour with the baking powder. Add the eggs and stir. Add milk gradually and olive oil. Add the grated cheese. Add any ingredients you like.

Here are a few good combinations:

  1. Sun-dried tomatoes plus a handful of fresh parsley, basil and chives

  2. Fresh goat cheese and diced and cooked leeks

  3. Olives and ham

  4. Salmon (smoked or/and fresh and cooked) plus spinach or courgettes

  5. Etc etc.

Bake for 45 minutes at 180ªC.
