Crêpe recipe¶

Makes 15

Ingredients

  • 125 g flour,

  • 25 cL milk,

  • 30 g butter,

  • 3 eggs,

  • salt.

Preparation

Put the flour in a bowl and add the beaten eggs in the middle. Mix and gradually add the milk until smooth. Then, add the melted butter and salt. Leave it to rest for 1 hour before cooking. If the batter is thick, you can add more milk or water or flat beer to it.
