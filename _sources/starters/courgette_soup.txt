Easy Courgette soup
===================
*Serves 4*

.. rubric:: Ingredients

- 800 g :index:`courgettes`, 
- 1 onion,
- :index:`parmesan cheese <cheese; parmesan>`,
- 400 mL :index:`chicken stock <stock; chicken>` (in can is better),
- 150 mL single cream,
- salt & pepper,
- nutmeg.

.. rubric:: Preparation

Slice the courgettes and onion thinly and put them in a pan with the chicken 
stock and bring it to a boil. Cover and bring heat down, and simmer for 15 
minutes. Then blend the soup, add the nutmeg, cheese and cream and stir. 
Season with salt and pepper. Serve while hot.
