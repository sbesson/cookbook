Parsnip and shallot soup
========================
*Serves 4*

.. rubric:: Ingredients

- 500 g :index:`parsnips <parsnip>`,
- 6 shallots,
- 2 tbsp olive oil,
- 500 mL vegetable stock,
- 1 grated :index:`orange rind <oranges>`,
- 100 mL crème fraiche,
- 1 tbsp wholegrain mustard,
- salt & pepper.

.. rubric:: Preparation

Heat the oil in a large saucepan, and fry the shallots gently for 3-4 minutes 
to soften. Peel the parsnips and cut into small chunks. Add to the pan and 
stir, then reduce the heat. Cover the pan tightly and leave on a low heat for 
about 15 minutes, shaking the pan so that it does not stick. Stir  in the 
stock.

Tip the contents of the pan into the blender or food processor and blend.
Return to the pan and stir the mustard, the orange rind and crème fraiche. 

Season, bring to the boil and serve.
