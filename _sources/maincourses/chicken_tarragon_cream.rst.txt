Chicken with tarragon cream
===========================
*Serves 4*

.. rubric:: Ingredients

- 4 :index:`chicken breasts <chicken; breasts>`,
- 1 bunch tarragon,
- 250 g butter, softened,
- 250 mL double cream,
- 2 :index:`lemons <lemon>`,
- salt & pepper.

.. rubric:: Preparation

Pre-heat the oven to 160Â°C.

Prepare the tarragon butter by chopping the tarragon finely and mixing it with 
the butter.

Put the chicken breasts in an ovenproof dish and coat with the tarragon 
butter. Bake in the oven for 20-30 minutes. Then carefully discard the melted 
butter, keeping the tarragon. Squeeze the lemon juice over the chicken 
breasts. Pour the double cream and season. Then bake for a further 15 minutes 
and serve with rice or couscous and a salad.
