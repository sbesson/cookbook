Apple and cinnamon ice cream
============================
*Serves 6-8*

.. rubric:: Ingredients

- 700 g :index:`apples`,
- 170 g brown sugar,
- 1 tbsp cinnamon,
- 850 mL single cream,
- 3 tbsp :index:`calvados <alcohol; calvados>` (whisky or cognac works too).

.. rubric:: Preparation

Peel the apples and slice them. Cook them slowly in a saucepan with 60 mL 
water, the sugar and the cinnamon. Then blend in a food processor. Whip the 
cream to make chantilly and add to the apple sauce. Add the alcohol and stir. 
Pour the cream into an ice-cream maker or in a metal dish and freeze.
