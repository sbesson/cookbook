Julie's chocolate cake
======================
*Serves 8*

.. rubric:: Ingredients

- 250 g :index:`dark chocolate <chocolate; dark>`,
- 125 g butter,
- 6 eggs,
- 250 g sugar,
- 2 tbsp milk,
- 4 tbsp flour.

.. rubric:: Preparation

Preheat oven to 180ÂºC.

In a saucepan, melt the chocolate with the milk. Melt the butter in. Remove 
from the heat and add the sugar, then the flour and the egg yolks. Beat the 
egg whites and fold them in the chocolate mixture gently. Cover a baking tray 
with foil and pour the cake mixture in. Cook for 45 minutes.
