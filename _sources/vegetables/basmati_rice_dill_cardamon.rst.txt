Basmati rice with dill and cardamon
===================================
*Serves 6*

.. rubric:: Ingredients

- 2 cups basmati or jasmine rice, 
- 2 tbsp olive oil,
- ½ tsp cinnamon powder,
- 5 cardamon pods crushed,
- ½ onion sliced into fine half rings or diced,
- 3 tbsp dried dill or 5 tbsp chopped fresh dill,
- 2 ½ cups :index:`chicken broth`,
- salt to season.

.. rubric:: Preparation

Wash the rice in water until clear. Drain, cover with cold water and soak for 
30 minutes. Pour the oil into a pan and set on medium heat. When the oil is 
hot, put in the cinnamon and cardamon. Stir for 5 seconds, then put the onions 
and stir-fry until golden-brown. Add the rice and the dill. Reduce the heat to 
medium-heat and stir until the rice grains look translucent (1-2 minutes). Add 
the chicken broth and bring to a boil. Cover tightly. Reduce the heat and cook 
for 25 minutes.
