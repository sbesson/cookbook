Divine chocolate frostingÂ¶

For one cake

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup butter,
  • 4 flat tbsp cocoa,
  • 1/4 cup milk,
  • 8 tbsp caster sugar,
  • 1 tsp vanilla,
  • 1 tbsp instant coffee (dissolved in 2 tbsp water).

Preparation

Boil the milk, butter and cocoa in a pan. Remove from heat and add the sugar, vanilla and coffee. Pour over the cake still hot!
