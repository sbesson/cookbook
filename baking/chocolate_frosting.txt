Divine chocolate frosting
=========================
*For one cake*

.. rubric:: Ingredients

- 1/3 cup butter,
- 4 flat tbsp :index:`cocoa`,
- 1/4 cup milk,
- 8 tbsp caster sugar,
- 1 tsp vanilla,
- 1 tbsp instant coffee (dissolved in 2 tbsp water).

.. rubric:: Preparation

Boil the milk, butter and cocoa in a pan. Remove from heat and add the sugar, 
vanilla and coffee. Pour over the cake still hot!
