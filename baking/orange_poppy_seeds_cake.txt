Orange and poppy seed cake
==========================
*Serves 8*

.. rubric:: Ingredients

- 50 g poppy seeds,
- 120 g soft butter,
- 185 mL warm milk,
- 250 g  self-raising flour,
- 250 g caster sugar,
- 185 g butter,
- 3 eggs,
- 1 packet vanilla sugar,
- 2 tbsp orange zest\ingredient{orange}.

.. rubric:: Topping:

- 150 g icing sugar,
- 2 tbsp orange juice,
- 1 tsp orange zest.

.. rubric:: Preparation

Preheat oven to 180ÂºC and grease a baking tray.  Mix the poppy seeds in a bowl 
with the warm milk and leave for 15 minutes.

Cream the sugar and butter together, then add the eggs, the flour and orange 
zest and mix. Incorporate the milk and poppy seeds and whisk well for 3 
minutes until the batter thickens. Pour in the cake tray and bake for 50 
minutes.

For the icing, melt the butter and mix with the icing sugar and orange zest 
and juice and 3 tablespoons of boiling water. Coat the cake with the icing 
while it is still warm.
